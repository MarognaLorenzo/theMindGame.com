# The Mind Online

The Mind Online is a browser-based multiplayer recreation of the cooperative card game *The Mind*. Players work together to clear levels by playing cards in ascending order without revealing their hands or talking about exact card values.

This project is a fan-made online recreation inspired by *The Mind* by Wolfgang Warsch.

Play for free at [the-mind-game.com](https://the-mind-game.com).

Original rules: [The Mind rulebook](https://cdn.1j1ju.com/medias/4c/1e/25-the-mind-rulebook.pdf).

## What The Game Is

This is a real-time co-op game for 2 or more players. Each round, every player receives a hand of hidden cards. The team then tries to play those cards from lowest to highest across the whole table, using timing, observation, and a little intuition.

The game is designed around trust and silence. You do not coordinate by naming numbers or comparing hands directly. Instead, you watch the pace of play and try to sense when it is safe to commit your card.

## How It Is Played

1. Create a lobby or join an existing one with a name.
2. Wait for the host to start the match.
3. In each level, every player gets cards equal to the current level number.
4. Players play cards one at a time in ascending order.
5. If a card is played too early, the team loses a life.
6. When all cards for a level are cleared, the team advances to the next level.

The game continues until the team clears the final level or runs out of lives.

## Rules Of The Game

- Cards must be played from lowest to highest across all players.
- No one can see the values in another player’s hand.
- You should not announce exact card values or force direct number comparisons.
- Wrong plays cost the team a life.
- When a life is lost, the team may also have cards removed from the table if the wrong play exposes lower cards.
- Certain levels reward the team with extra lives or shurikens.
- Shurikens are limited power-ups that can be used during a run to clear cards from the table.

## Lives And Shurikens

The team starts each run with a number of lives based on the lobby size. A wrong play reduces the shared pool of lives, and if the team reaches zero lives, the run ends in defeat. When a card is played and is not the lowest one across all players, team mates with cards that are lower than the played card can discard their cards.

Shurikens are a limited recovery tool. The run starts with one shuriken, and some levels reward an additional one. They can stack up to a cap of three, so saving them for a difficult moment can turn the round around. Playing a shuriken allows every player discard their lowest card.

## Win And Loss

You win by clearing every level up to the target level for that lobby size. You lose if the team runs out of lives before finishing the run.

## Notes

- The game runs entirely in the browser.
- No installation or deployment is required to play.
- A stable internet connection is recommended for multiplayer sessions.
